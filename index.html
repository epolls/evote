Why? there are significant costs in printing and manageing paper based ballot systems. Online voting eliminates such expenses by migrating ballots to a secure voting portal, where members can cast votes stress-free, and without the existence of double voting. Members can access the easy-to-use online voting portals through their computers, smartphones, or tablets, wherever they are. Voting is literally just a matter of a few clicks. The system offers transparency, auditable, immutable, anonymous voting experience that will make every individual to have voting will.

Immutable and Anonymous

The voting system makes voting 100% secure and immutable. Voter anonymity is guaranteed by transparent system architecture.

Easy to organize, easy to vote

Creating a vote is intuitive, easy and fast. No coding knowledge is required. Voting can be conducted on the go on a laptop, desktop, smartphone or tablet.

Transparent and auditable

One of the main characteristics depicted in the voting system is its transparency. Total voting transparency is guaranteed.
